For this report I have evaluated the usability of Nebula Knights. For this I interviewed 4 different people and gave questionnaires to a further 20 people. Nebula Knights is a 3rd Person isometric hack and slash game. The player uses a controller or Gamepad to control the movement of the players and attack the enemies. Due to the Couch Co-op nature of the game it allowed me to get 4 people playing the game simultaneously.

In general the interviews and questionnaires acquired some useful feedback that could be used to take the game further from a usability point of view. I used this data to find out many trends from how much the players liked the way the controls were portrayed to how much they liked the storyline of the games to determine where the game falls short and where it excels. In this webpage I will describe how I reached my results and what my results where.

James Collins - 2017
